About the Recycling Hive

Recycling Hive is a place to check out what can be recycled locally to you - above and beyond what your council refuse service collects.

It is a service that relies entirely on being updated by the local community - i.e. the other members of your Hive.

If you're here to find out what you can recycle locally, just head straight to Search Hive and get browsing!

Or, if you're a local recycling collector, then visiting Add Collection will allow you to get started telling everyone what you can take from them.

If you're looking for a more in-depth explanation, then the FAQs have got you covered.
